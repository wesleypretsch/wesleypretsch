Secured By

Secured By is the first student-led organization at NC State University dedicated to cybersecurity education and awareness on campus. Our goal is to teach the importance of cybersecurity and spread the word of important topics in the field. We make sure to educate others in a way so that no matter one's background, technical or not, everyone can learn something new! Check out our website!

February 2021 I co-founded Secured By with Scott Joyner and Jared Forbes. Since then we have gained a great traction on campus and have 80+ members in our Discord! Each month we hold a Keynote Event where a professional from the cybersecurity community comes in to give a keynote speech on a security realted topic. We've had topics such at IoT, Defense Tactics, and 5G! While the Keynotes have been our main form of events on campus, we plan on moving forward by hosting student-focused events and building relationships with professional organizations in Raleigh!

Shown above is a promotional video from our March Keynote Event featuring Dr. Jeff Crume! He gave a speech on IoT security to an audience of 25 students in-person and 30+ people online via Twitch!

My Travels

I've had the incredible privilage to travel the world and I want to share some of my favorite trips.

Bonaire

Home to some of the world's most beautiful coral reefs, Bonaire was one of the best SCUBA diving trips I've ever had. I went with my dad during the summer of 2019 and in the course of four days, we made 16 different dives, two in the morning and two at night. Bonaire was a true diver's paradise and I'd love to go back.

Keet Seel

Keet Seel is one of the most preserved Anasazi ruins in the world. It's a 17 mile hike into the Navajo National Monument, only 20 people are allowed into the canyon per day, and the strenuous hike involves a 600 foot elevation change, quicksand, and the entire trail made of silt. I completed the hike with my dad and it was by far the most difficult, yet rewarding hike I have ever done.

Zion

On the same trip as Keet Seel, my dad and I also hiked through Zion National Park. Zion offers stunning views of the vast canyons and wildlife. My favorite part of that trip watching watching the sun set in Zion.

France

Summer of 2016 my family and I visited Paris, France. Paris is such a beautiful city with loads of historical landmarks. Some of my favorite parts were seeing the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Art Museum, and Arc de Triomphe.

Guatemala

In 2015, my dad and I visited Guatemala. My favorite part was exploring the ancient Mayan ruins of Tikal. This is a picture of me infront of the most iconic structure, The Great Jaguar Temple.

Michigan

My dad took me salmon fishing in Traverse City, Michigan, spring 2013. We spent the weekend there, fishing in the morning and eating fresh cherries from the farmer's market by night. This is picture of my dad and I with the 18 lb salmon I caught!

Germany

In November of 2012 me, my dad, and my older sister visited Germany for five days. We visited Frankfurt and Berlin, visiting all of the small towns in between. This is a picture of my dad and I infront of Neuschwanstein Castle.

China

I went to China when I was 8 years old with my dad. While it was a long time ago, I remember every detail. We visited Shanghai, Xian, and Beijing; taking in the incredible Chinese culture and seeing some of China's most famous destinations such as the Great Wall, the Terrecotta Army, and the Forbidden City.
